Why I build community

Soft skills, in a hard-skill field.

A hard subject, clearly explained, is one of the most powerful things you can offer someone. In a field that prizes technical depth, the ability to make complex things legible across very different kinds of rooms is its own form of expertise — and it's one no org chart can give you or take away.

That's why I keep building rooms: for the scientists keeping current, the mid-career professionals reaching for leadership, and anyone trying to come out of a transition more current, not less.
