Speaker bio

Copy-ready, third person.

Celine G. Han is a translational data scientist and AI strategist working at the intersection of oncology clinical development, real-world evidence, and machine learning. As an Associate Director of Translational Data Science at GSK, she leverages agentic AI/ML across clinical development and leads on AI strategy and partnerships. Her career spans the research bench, clinical and translational data science at Dana-Farber and Bristol Myers Squibb, and data & AI business development — a dual-lens view from both sides of the partnership table. She founded and leads the Bioinformatics & AI XChange, a community for scientists keeping current in a fast-moving field, and is known for making hard subjects clearly explained.
